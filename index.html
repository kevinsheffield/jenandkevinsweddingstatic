about us

BRIDE

Jennifer Lauren Tears

I was not really looking to meet anyone, especially at a party. As cynical as it may sound, it was just my being practical. It was the end of my second to last semester, and I would soon be off to face what would become the bane of my existence... law school.

Anyway, I almost did not make it to the party; the person who was to be our designated driver decided that the game of "pulling straws" was unfair. (We had drawn straws to determine who was to be the evening's d.d.). I am not sure how it could have been anymore fair? However, after what seemed like an eternity of whining, from a grown man, mind you, the d.d. was soon "convinced" to act as such. We finally arrived.

Call it fate, call it destiny, Kev and I were meant to meet at this party. During a game of flip cup, and influenced by a cup or two of "liquid courage," I began what I had initially intended to be smack talk with a man dressed to the "tee". I became particularly intrigued by this man who was as equally able in the smack talk as I. Soon thereafter, what had begun as smack talk soon turned into flirting. I have never been so forward with a man as I was with Kev... I actually invited myself on a "date" with him. There was just something about him that made him worth the effort... made him worth moving beyond what I believed to be my comfort zone. I was absolutely smitten!

Our first date was the longest date I have ever been on. Not that it was boring... it was not boring by any means, but it lasted well over 10 hours. I felt comfortable with Kevin, I felt the need to be around him at all times. Our relationship grew exponentially over the course of a few months, but I always had a lingering fear... that we would go our separate ways upon graduation. However, I should have never been fearful. Kevin loved me more than anything in the world and would follow me to the ends of the earth as he has done and continues to do. I love him with equal passion and I only wish I could show him how grateful I am for his loving and wanting to love me.

Kev is my rock, my unyielding support, my love, and my best friend. As cliche as it sounds, he completes me! I continue to love him more and more each day and I cannot wait to spend my life with him as his wife.

GROOM

Kevin Randall Sheffield

There she was. It was just a party with some guys I played poker with often, but there she was. She is playing what seemed to be the longest game of beer pong ever. I still remember seeing that amazing smile for the first time. For some reason it was decided that everyone should play a game of flip cup with guys versus girls being the teams. I notice she is captaining the women's team and hurry to go stand next to her. She starts talking smack to me and the games commence. They go by way too quickly, but I make the most of it getting to know her (in between smack talk of my own of course). After, one of her friends felt the need to introduce her to me several times, as if I would or even could forget her by then. We danced and talked the night away, well at least until her roommates decided to leave the party and wouldn't leave her in the hands of a stranger.

Luckily she had already invited herself to free beer and nachos the following day. I couldn't let that be our first date, so we also went to a movie and then dinner after. It might have been a long date, but I enjoyed every second of it. She was already done with exams for the fall semester, but she stayed in Gainesville just to spend time with me.

Finals, studying, senior project... the last few months of school certainly weren't easy even without her gumming up the works. I hated leaving her and couldn't wait to return. Every college grad has to make the decision of "where to now?", for me it was decided on a "reading day" many months prior.

She gave me courage that night.

A shy computer nerd like me doesn't approach the girl; he certainly doesn't get the girl. He doesn't move halfway across the country; he doesn't travel halfway across the world. For 7 years she has been by my side, taking on the world with me. We have done so much in those years, and I can't wait to see what the next 80 have in store for us.

I've always been a sucker for a brown eyed, punk rock girl,
and for her I'd shoot the world.
